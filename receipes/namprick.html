Namprick Sauce - The best thai sauce for seafood

    Ingrediants

  • 3-4 thai bird's eye chilies
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon shrimp paste
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar

    Preperation

  1. roast chilli and garlic till fragrant
  2. pound roasted chilies and garlic into a coarse paste
  3. add shrimp paste and mix well
  4. stir in fish sauce, tamarind paste, lime juice, and sugar
  5. serve with assorted seafood
