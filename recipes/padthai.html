Pad Thai - Thai stir-fried noodle dish

    Ingrediants

  • 8 oz (225g) rice noodles
  • 5 medium-sized shrimp
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 3-4 green onions
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts

    Pad Thai Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • tablespoons palm sugar

    Preperation

  1. Soak rice noodles in hot water until tender; drain.
  2. Stir-fry shrimp/tofu, garlic, and eggs in oil.
  3. Add noodles, tamarind sauce, fish sauce, and sugar; toss.
  4. sMix in bean sprouts and green onions.
  5. Serve with crushed peanuts and lime wedges. Enjoy!
